<p>Themed “Empowering yourself, Inspiring Others”, the 2025 SG100WIT List
celebrated 100 Women in Tech and 25 Girls in Tech for their exceptional contributions to Singapore's digital economy.</p>
<p>From AI governance and cybersecurity to blockchain and computational science, our honourees represent the breadth of tech leadership driving cross-disciplinary innovation across public and private sectors. </p>
<p>The unveiling of SG100WIT 2025 reinforces the role of inclusive leadership in future-proofing Singapore’s digital standing.</p>
